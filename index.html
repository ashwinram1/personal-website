About Me

I'm a Software Engineer primarily experienced with and interested in back-end development, databases, automation, cloud computing, and transforming data into useful and insightful information. I have a true passion for creativity, curiosity, and a keen attention to detail. I love finding different mediums through which I can manifest these attributes. The main one is programming and software development. Back when I learned computer programming, I fell in love with it, and it is what inspired me to study Computer Science and pursue a career as a Software Engineer! However I am inspired by it beyond just my career, as I use programming in my everyday tasks to automate monotonous tasks, and to create projects that are personally useful or interesting to me. Other creative and artistic mediums which I have found to love as side-hobbies include DJing and barbering. Both of these require me to pay very close attention to detail in order to excel in them, and both offer me freedom to tinker, explore, and discover new things-- which is what I enjoy doing the most!
